engaging

Pushing one-way messages at your market only works if anyone is listening. Sadly, with all the noise out there, few people are. And besides, who looks at advertising or PR anymore? News flash: No one ever looked at advertising and few ever trusted spin. But people do notice what interests or inspires them. Creating powerful, motivating 'conversations and stories' to engage employees and customers equally is how brands grow from awareness to advocacy.

Research that reveals genuine gaps and opportunities, audience-relevant value propositions, brand stories and design that compel an emotive response, and communications that inspire action are Tenzing's tools of mass and micro disruption. The engagement that happens as a result is the foundation of sales, donations or any other quantitative metric you wish to apply.
