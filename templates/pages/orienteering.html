Tenzing Orienteering works from the inside out to help stakeholders discover a mutually achievable path to brand delivery. It's like a series of revival meetings for staff, leadership and influencers. The conversations are grounded in research, confidential if required, often blunt, and always engaging. We inspire brand ownership and then guide the human effort required to go to market as one. Tenzing Orienteering is to facilitation what Jackson Pollock is to paint-by-numbers.

Tenzing's Orienteering process has been applied to public organizations like The City of Mississauga and Bruce County, healthcare organizations including Hamilton Health Sciences and The Canadian Mental Health Association, and in the private sector with companies such as Baker and McKenzie LLP, CGA Canada and SNC Lavalin. Orienteering is the first step in mobilizing your human channel.
