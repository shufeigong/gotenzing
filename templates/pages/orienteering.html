Tenzing Orienteering works from the inside out to help stakeholders discover a mutually achievable path to brand delivery. It's like a series of revival meetings for staff, leadership and influencers. The conversations are grounded in research, confidential if required, often blunt, and always engaging. We inspire brand ownership and then guide the human effort required to go to market as one. Tenzing Orienteering is to facilitation what Jackson Pollock is to paint-by-numbers.

Tenzing was hired to help Ontario's Bruce County position for sustainable entrepreneurial growth. We could have done focus groups and produced an ad campaign. We engaged the existing business communities of Bruce in a spirited conversation instead, and created eHarmony for prospective business owners as a result.

The Hamilton Health Sciences executive team was proud of their new strategic plan and hired Tenzing to brand it for 10,000 employees. Our Orienteering process revealed a very unreceptive audience - the managers, professionals and staff at the hospital had not been consulted. We helped re-create the strategy based on hospital-wide input and it became a huge success.
